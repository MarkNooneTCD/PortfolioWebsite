Renderhood

Renderhood was one of my own small ideas. Still under significant development, the aim of Renderhood was to bring together the idea of distributed computing and allow the Renderhood website to render out 3D CGI movie frames for both professionals and amateurs. The aim here is to bring people closer and feel they have helped out lower budget CGI film studios. Renting and operating servers can be very expensive so allowing people to come together and donating there CPU cycles would hopefully remove a strong barrier for amateur studios.
