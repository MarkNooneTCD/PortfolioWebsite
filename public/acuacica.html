Acuacica

Acuacica was a system designed to track your shower usage statistics. Be it shower temperatures over the week, cost of heating the water or even how long your showers have taken, this application keeps track of it all. This project was given to myself and several other collegues by a Trinity startup. Starting from the idea we had 10 weeks to develop a full production ready app for our Software Engineering module. This web app was developed in Angular, with a backend running Node.Js and MongoDB. Please stay tuned to my GitHub to check out the final product in the coming weeks!
