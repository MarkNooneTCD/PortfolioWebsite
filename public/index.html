Hi I'm Mark.


I am an undergraduate Computer Scientist studying in Trinity College, Dublin. I love bringing ideas straight from the little seed it starts off as, all the way through to the production ready application. As a seasoned developer, I always enjoy working with a good design which is what kickstarted my interest in UI/UX.

Please take a look at my work below. Although it is somewhat limited currently, I look to continue improving on my new found hobby and grow as a designer. Thank you for visiting!

Work + Projects

Acuacica

Acuacica was a system designed to track your shower usage statistics. Be it shower temperatures over the week, cost of heating the water or even how long your showers have taken, this application keeps track of it all.

Renderhood

Renderhood was one of my own small ideas. Still under development, the aim of Renderhood was to bring together the idea of distributed computing and allow the Renderhood website to render out 3D CGI movie frames for both professionals and amateurs.

Syze

Syze is another trinity startup that aims to partner your phone and machine learning in order to get your clothing measurements. They needed a small brochure website that could be extended to include their future application plans and this was the result.

Cj's Gaff

This is a small project currently undergoing development. Temporarily named after the CSB student himself, CJ's Gaff attempts to create a truly competitive clothing store, with each garment competeting against the other.

Stoury

Stoury was the project that launched me into launchbox. Brought on by two trinity students, Stoury was a full e-tourism platform designed to allow locals to enlist their own private tours.
